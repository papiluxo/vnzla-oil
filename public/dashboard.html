Critical Context

REVENUE CONTROLEO 14373: All oil revenue settles in US-controlled Treasury accounts. $1B+ collected (mid-Feb). $5B projected near-term. $150B potential over 10yr. GL-52 (Mar 18) broadened to all established US entities. GL-56 (Apr 14) authorizes contingent contract negotiations.
